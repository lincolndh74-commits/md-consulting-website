What We Do

Full Range of Services

From compliance and registration to HR and payroll, we provide end-to-end support for security companies

Security Officer Grading

We process all grades for security officers to ensure your team is properly certified and meets the requirements set by PSIRA and industry standards. Whether upgrading or registering new officers, we manage the full grading process.

Renewals

Stay current with all necessary renewals for your security business and officers. We track expiry dates and manage the renewal process to ensure uninterrupted operations and continued compliance.

Business Registration

Get your security company properly registered and set up from the start. We handle all the paperwork and processes required to establish your business as a legal, compliant entity in the security sector.

Employment Contracts & Files

Comprehensive employee file management including properly drafted employment contracts, personal documentation, PSIRA numbers, ID numbers, company numbers, and firearm registration details.

Code of Conduct

Professional code of conduct documentation that sets clear expectations for your security staff. We draft and implement conduct standards that protect both employer and employee interests.

Employer-Employee Undertakings

Formal undertaking agreements between employer and employees that clearly define responsibilities, expectations, and mutual obligations within the workplace.

Statements & Warnings

Professionally prepared statements and warning documentation for disciplinary matters. We ensure all warnings follow proper procedure and are legally compliant for your protection.

Employment History Management

Complete employment history record keeping including PSIRA numbers, ID numbers, company registration numbers, and firearm details for all personnel in your organisation.

Compliance & Dismissal Records

Thorough record keeping for compliance matters and dismissal procedures. We maintain detailed records that protect your business in the event of disputes or audits.

Acknowledgement of Debts

Proper documentation for all financial obligations including uniform deductions, salary advances, and fines. Clear, legally sound agreements that protect both parties.

Notice to Appear & Dismissals

Formal notice to appear documentation and dismissal procedures handled with legal precision. We ensure every step of the disciplinary process is properly documented and procedurally fair.

Recruitment

Full recruitment services for new permanent staff and relievers. We help you find, screen, and onboard qualified security personnel to fill your operational requirements.

Payroll Reminders

Timely month-end payroll reminders and administration support to ensure your staff are paid accurately and on time, every time. We help manage the payroll preparation process.

PSIRA Liaison

Direct management of your PSIRA portal for all engagements and disengagements. When staff resign, are dismissed, or terminated, we update the portal immediately. When new staff join, we add them seamlessly.
