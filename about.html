Meet Our Director

Ms Mary Naidoo is the founder and director of M.D Consulting & Training (PTY) Ltd, a company dedicated to providing expert administrative and compliance services to the private security industry.

Based in Kharwastan, Durban, Mary brings hands-on experience in managing the complex regulatory environment that security companies operate within. Her expertise spans PSIRA compliance, human resources management, recruitment, and the full spectrum of employment administration.

Mary's personal approach ensures that every client receives dedicated attention. She manages PSIRA portal engagements and disengagements directly, ensuring seamless staff transitions whether employees are joining, resigning, or being terminated.

About M.D Consulting & Training

M.D Consulting & Training (PTY) Ltd (Ck No: K2021669425) is a registered South African company providing specialised consulting services to the security sector.

We understand that running a security company requires more than just providing guards on the ground. The administrative, regulatory, and HR demands can be overwhelming. That's where we step in.

Our comprehensive suite of services covers everything from initial business registration and security officer grading to ongoing payroll management, PSIRA compliance, and disciplinary procedures. We serve as your complete back-office partner, allowing you to focus on growing your security operations.
